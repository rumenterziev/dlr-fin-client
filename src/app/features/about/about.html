About

Hi, I'm Rumen Terziev.

A full-stack developer focused on building efficient, scalable and user-focused web applications. I love turning rough ideas into polished products.

My story

My journey started with a free online Java course at SoftUni — and turned into a career. Since then I've earned a diploma in Software Engineering and spent nearly three years shipping production code across a variety of projects, growing both technically and as a teammate.

What I do

I specialize in Java, Spring Boot, Angular, Python and MySQL, with production deployments on Microsoft Azure. I work daily with Docker, Jenkins, Git, Redis and Jira. Outside of work, I keep building full-stack pet projects from scratch — front to back — to stay sharp.
